How to use the augmented reality cabling template

Some diagrams provide access to an AR template. You can hold this template over the server to verify cabling and cabling routing.

  1. Hold your device in landscape mode.

  2. Click the Launch AR template button.

    The device's back camera is activated and the AR template appears on the screen.

  3. Hold the template over the server.

  4. To view the AR template full screen, swipe up on the image to hide the browser controls.
